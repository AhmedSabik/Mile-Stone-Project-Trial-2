About RockThePalace..

Over the past two decades RockThePalace have established themselves as true trendsetters in rock music. They've been nominated for various awards, toured the globe with iconic pop and rock music icons, and sang for HM the queen.

Bring a touch of the sixties to your wedding, event or private party.

Jon...

is a soulful talent who has been vagabonding around the UK for 25 years, stalking Brighton’s local watering holes monthly with residencies around the city. Swinging his way through weddings, club nights, festivals, corporate parties, diners and dives; the band are the perfect choice for those looking for something special.

Chad...

plays anything from the obvious, the unusual and the surprisingly modern. With a back catalogue booming full of 50-60s Rock ‘n’ Roll and R ‘n’ B classics side by side with old school hip hop, pop hits, original tracks and 90s nostalgia. The Brighton based rock 'n roller serves up all kinds of songs straight off the grill.

Dan...

prides himself on his shape shifting ability. He's able to scrub up for a wedding or scrub down for a club night. His work features blistering piano solos and the timeless sound of the double bass. His classic sound of the 50's appeals to all ages, getting all of your guests jiving on the dance floor long into the night.

Tim...

is an excellent pianist whose amazing playing needs to be seen to be believed. Inspired by the likes of Jerry Lee Lewis and Jools Holland his expertly crafted solos will leave your guests amazed. He has years of combined experience and has played at over 50 events in the last year alone, including weddings and parties.
